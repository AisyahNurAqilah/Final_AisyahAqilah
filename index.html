HISTORY


Roselle (Hibiscus Sabdariffa), is a shrub belonging to the Malvaceae family. In Iran, it is typically known as sour tea. In English-speaking countries it is called Red Sorrel. Originally from Africa and now widely naturalized in tropical and subtropical regions of the world particularly in India and Southeast Asia.
Smoothies Roselle Juice were founded on March 15, 2019 by our founder Aisyah Nur Aqilah Nasir. She got an idea to start her business after knowing the kindness and goodness of Roselle for health withiout age level.     Process


    4 steps of making roselle juice

    Let's Learn and Make Together

    STEP 1

    The workers needed to harvest the flowers then peel the petals (calyces) off. Just used the calyx part of the flower and removes the seed pod. Once all roselle flowers have done for de-seed podded, it need to clean them off with regular drinking water and get some water boiling in a pot.

    STEP 2

    The roselle calyxes need to wash again before starting to boil. This was to ensure there was no soil or dirt on them. Once the flowers were in the boiling water, add a pinch of salt to helps the balance out the natural bitterness.

    STEP 3

    After 10 to 15 minutes, the boiled roselles calyx needs to be filtered for gets it water.
